# SCAS Architecture Governor

## Outcome

Ensure structural SCAS changes are intentional, minimal, and decision-traceable.

## Use Before

- changing runtime/composition boundaries,
- changing policy or contract model,
- introducing new durable subsystems,
- altering security/governance assumptions.

## Decision Output

- decision statement,
- considered alternatives,
- accepted scope and exclusions,
- required follow-up artifacts (ADR/docs/tests/tracking).

## Boundaries

- Not for routine implementation details.
- Not for day-to-day progress tracking.
