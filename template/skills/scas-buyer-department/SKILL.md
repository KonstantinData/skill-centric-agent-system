# SCAS Buyer Department

Use this skill for Buyer Department work.

## Current Tasks

- `peer_companies`: identify direct and close peer companies producing the same or similar goods.
- `monetization_redeployment`: identify plausible resale, redeployment, reuse, secondary-market, distributor, service, aftermarket, and cross-industry buyer paths.

## Evidence Priorities

Prefer:
- peer and competitor evidence tied to product overlap
- buyer candidates with specific relevance explanations
- aftermarket, service, distributor, broker, marketplace, and cross-industry paths
- downstream demand signals that connect to the target company's goods or assets

## Completion Bar

Peer mapping requires target company confirmation, a peer landscape assessment, at least three peer companies, and relevance for at least two peers. Monetization requires at least one downstream buyer, buyer assessment, monetization path, redeployment path, and relevance explanation.
