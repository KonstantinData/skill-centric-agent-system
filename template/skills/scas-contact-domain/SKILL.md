# SCAS Contact Domain

Use this skill for Contact domain package work.

## Current Tasks

- `target_company_contacts`: identify decision-makers and likely meeting stakeholders at the target company.
- `contact_discovery`: identify publicly visible decision-makers at prioritized buyer firms.
- `contact_qualification`: qualify contacts by seniority, function, buying-center role, verification quality, target-case relevance, and outreach angle.

## Evidence Priorities

Prefer:
- company leadership pages
- LinkedIn-visible public profile snippets
- press releases and conference pages
- procurement, operations, supply chain, asset management, aftermarket, finance, or divisional leadership signals

## Completion Bar

Target-company contacts require at least two contacts and a target-company summary. Buyer-firm discovery requires at least three contacts, coverage quality, and verified company names. Qualification requires prioritized contacts, narrative summary, seniority, and outreach angle.
